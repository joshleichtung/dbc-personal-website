Mascis the Dog

Origin Story

I found my dog Mascis when he was only 3 months old. He was eating the crumbs out of a bag of chips in a gutter. We picked him up and tried to find his owner around the neighborhood. Eventually, we were introduced to a man named Icewater, who was Mascis's owner.

Due to circumstances beyond his control, Icewater was unable to care for Mascis, and offered to sell him to us if we took good care of him. A deal was struck, and Mascis came home for a bath, an life necessity that hte hates to this day.

Mascis is named after shaggy-haired, bearded guitar player named J Mascis, founder of Dinosaur Jr.
